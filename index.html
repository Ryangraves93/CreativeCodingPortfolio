Ryan Graves Portfolio - Creative Computing

| Term Progression

During the first term of my creative computing module, I explored implementations of
transformations and translations using trigonometry functions, such as inverse tangents (Atan2), sine & cosine.
During the term, what interested me the most was primitive vertex rendering, so as a result the sketches
displayed below, centre around vertex transformations and rotations use these implementations.

legends

Develping on the previous concept of the HSB grid, the sketch is a color wheel made of triangle fans. The triangle fan segments are mapped to x position of the mouse.

  • Coordinates are calculated by getting the sine & cosine of the angle and multiplying it by the desired radius
  • Iterator on the loop is mapped to value of the x position of the mouse
